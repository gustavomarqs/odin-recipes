Carrot Cake

Carrot cake is a very popular dessert in our country, especially for breakfast and afternoon tea. With its irresistible chocolate frosting and its soft and moist interior, it is a recipe that conquers everyone's heart! See how to make carrot cake with us, a practical recipe, prepared with the help of a blender and perfect to enjoy with a cup of coffee!

Batter

  • 1/2 cup (tea) of oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 and 1/2 cups (tea) of wheat flour
  • 3 medium carrots grated
  • 2 cups (tea) of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

Frosting

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 cup (tea) of sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 1 cup (tea) of milk

Instructions

  1. In a blender, add the carrot, eggs, and oil, then mix.
  2. Add the sugar and beat again for 5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl or mixer, add the wheat flour and then mix again.
  4. Add the baking powder and mix slowly with a spoon.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 180Â° C for approximately 40 minutes.
  6. Pour into a bowl the butter, cocoa powder, sugar, and milk, then mix.
  7. Bring the mixture to the heat and continue mixing until you get a creamy consistency, then pour the frosting over the cake.
